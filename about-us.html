About Habitat

Hi Everyone,

As some of you are aware, I am travelling to Malawi next summer for two weeks, to build two houses, for two orphaned and vulnerable families.

My girlfriend Tash volunteered in Malawi with Habitat for Humanity last year and I was so inspired by her account of the amazing work that’s being done, that I decided to get on board for the summer 2019 trip. We will travel together, alongside a team of other volunteers to support Habitat’s work in Malawi. The programme builds simple homes, facilitates training on HIV and Malaria prevention, and encourages will writing. This protects children and their families, providing them with a solid foundation to build a brighter future.

Habitat for Humanity is making a huge difference to the lives of many people around the world. Through sustainable development, they are giving people a “a hand up, not a hand out”. As I’m sure you can imagine, I am tremendously excited about the prospect of being a part of their work on the ground and would greatly appreciate your help.

I need to raise a total of £2400 before I go. I will be making my own donation to cover flights/ other personal costs. This means that any money raised through donations will go towards the actual build or be fed into other Habitat projects in Malawi.

To find out more, please feel free to explore this site.
