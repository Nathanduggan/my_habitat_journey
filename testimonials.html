Testimonials



"My trip to Malawi 2018 was truly life altering. The Malawian people are so friendly and it is no wonder the country is known as the "The warm heart of Africa" We worked in Gojo village where every person we met welcomed us into their community with embraces and huge smiles on their faces. I was provileged to be a part of a team of amazing individuals."

Natasha Thompson, Habitat volunteer 2018

"My trip to Malawi with Habitat for Humanity was such a joy from start to finish as the Malawian people are so warm, happy and welcoming. The way Habitat work with Communities to identify and support the most vulnerable, and with volunteers to coordinated safe and life changing trips is exemplary."

Sinead Kelly , Habitat volunteer and team leader 2018
